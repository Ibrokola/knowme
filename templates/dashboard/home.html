Welcome to knowMe...

What is knowMe?

KnowMe is a fun matchmaking platform where you connect directly with people you share common values with. This is determined through algorithm which asks series of random questions to determine match percentages.

So what does knowMe help you achieve?

The aim of knowMe is to keep you curious about who you share common values with, the more value you share the more the match percentage increases and lots more

Give it a shot, let us know what we need to improve upon and we hope you have fun.
